    WHAT WE
    DETECT

    QR codes are increasingly weaponized. QR Shield catches them all.

    🎣
    PHISHING SITES

    Fake banking, payment, and login pages designed to steal credentials via convincing QR code redirects.

    HIGH RISK
    ☣️
    MALWARE PAYLOADS

    QR codes that trigger automatic APK downloads, drive-by installs, or malicious scripts on scan.

    HIGH RISK
    🔀
    REDIRECT CHAINS

    Multi-hop redirect sequences that obscure the final malicious destination from casual inspection.

    Lookalike domains mimicking trusted brands — e.g. paypa1.com, amaz0n-secure.net — caught by heuristics.
